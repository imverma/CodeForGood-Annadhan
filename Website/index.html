Annadhan

‘What’s left is not over’!

Annadhan is a food rescue organization that focuses on the principle of ‘What’s left is not over’! It is primarily into collection and distribution of excess edible and untouched food. Annadhan believes that there is enough food to feed everyone. By reducing the burden to grow more to feed people, they believe that there is an important role played in conserving the essentials of our depleting agricultural land.
